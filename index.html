About InAmigos Foundation

InAmigos Foundation, founded on September 23, 2020, by Mr. Govind Shukla (Founder & CEO), is a Section 8 registered non-profit organization committed to creating lasting social impact.

With its base in Bilaspur, Chhattisgarh, the foundation operates across India, addressing critical societal issues through a network of dedicated professionals and volunteers.

The foundation's mission is to serve the community compassionately and its vision is to create opportunities for every underprivileged person to grow with dignity and respect.

Meet Our Founder

GS

Govind Shukla

Founder & CEO — InAmigos Foundation

Mr. Govind Shukla founded InAmigos Foundation on September 23, 2020, with a vision to bridge societal gaps where government and communities fall short. Under his leadership, the foundation has grown into a pan-India movement with 93,000+ LinkedIn followers and 30,000+ trained interns.

His leadership has brought together thousands of volunteers, professionals and corporate partners to drive meaningful change across India — from feeding the hungry to empowering women and protecting the environment.

Our Projects

Six flagship initiatives addressing India's most critical social challenges

🍱

Project SEVA

Food & Clothing

Over 50,000+ meals and clothing distributed to underprivileged communities. Volunteers distribute freshly prepared meals to daily wage workers, homeless people and families in need — especially during extreme weather conditions.

  • ✅ Food distribution drives
  • ✅ Clothing donation camps
  • ✅ Relief during disasters
📚

Project Bachpanshala

Education

Bridging educational gaps by training underprivileged children in basic digital literacy, life skills, and school education support in rural India.

  • ✅ Digital literacy training
  • ✅ School supplies & scholarships
  • ✅ Mentorship programs
🐾

Project Jeev

Animal Welfare

Dedicated to the welfare of stray and abandoned animals, feeding 50+ animals daily through our volunteer network while advocating for their rights and humane treatment.

  • ✅ Daily animal feeding
  • ✅ Medical care & shelter
  • ✅ Animal rights awareness
👩

Project Udaan

Women Empowerment

Empowering 900+ women through skill development workshops, financial literacy, entrepreneurship opportunities and menstrual hygiene awareness in collaboration with rural self-help groups.

  • ✅ Skill development workshops
  • ✅ Financial independence
  • ✅ Digital awareness campaigns
🌱

Project Prakriti

Environment

Advocating for sustainability by planting 20,000+ saplings, organizing clean-up drives, waste management initiatives and promoting eco-friendly agriculture across India.

  • ✅ Tree plantation drives
  • ✅ Waste management
  • ✅ Eco-friendly farming
🚀

Project Vikas

Skill Development

Facilitating employment and skill development through internships in various fields, having trained over 30,000+ youth interns across India — building the next generation of professionals.

  • ✅ Professional internships
  • ✅ Digital skill training
  • ✅ Career development
